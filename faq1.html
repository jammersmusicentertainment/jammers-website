Instrumental & Beat FAQ

Everything you need to know about our production process.

1. What is an instrumental service?

This is where we create the background music (beats/arrangement) for your song, advertisement, or video. It does not include vocals unless specified in a bundle.

2. Can I request a specific genre?

Definitely! We specialize in several styles including:

  • Hip-hop / Trap / R&B
  • Pop / Acoustic
  • Worship / Gospel
  • Cinematic / Background Music

3. How long does it take to finish one track?

Standard turnaround time is 3 to 7 days, depending on the complexity of the arrangement. Rush orders can be negotiated.

4. Do I own the beat after payment?

By default, you get a Lifetime Commercial License. This means you can use it anywhere forever. However, "Full Ownership" (Buyout) is a separate option if you want to own the composition rights entirely.

5. What files will I receive?

You will receive high-quality WAV and MP3 formats. Track stems (individual instrument tracks) are available upon request for an additional fee.
