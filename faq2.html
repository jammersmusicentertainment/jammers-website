Frequently Asked Questions

How should I send my vocal tracks?

Please send them as raw "dry" files (no effects) in WAV format. Make sure they are recorded with good levels and no clipping.

What is the turnaround time?

Usually 2 to 4 days per song for editing. Full production might take 5 to 10 days depending on the project scope.

Do you offer discounts for EPs or Albums?

Yes! We have special rates for bulk projects (3 or more songs). Message us to get a custom quote.
