What exactly is a "Business Jingle"?

A jingle is more than just a song. It is a strategic audio tool designed for brand recall. Our jingle package includes original lyrics, lead vocals, and a custom instrumental created specifically to represent your business or campaign.

What is the "Standard License"?

Our standard license gives you Lifetime Commercial Usage. You can use the audio for social media ads, YouTube, TV/Radio commercials, and in-store promotions without ever paying us again. Ownership stays with Jammers to keep rates affordable.

Full Rights Buyout?

If you want to own the song 100%—meaning you own the copyright, the master recording, and you can sell the song—you can opt for the Full Rights Buyout.
